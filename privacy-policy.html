Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MILOE ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the MILOE mobile application ("Service"). It also explains your rights under applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), where relevant.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

Definitions

  • Account: A unique account created for you to access our Service (note: MILOE does not require an account).
  • Application: MILOE, the software application provided by the Company.
  • Company: MILOE.
  • Country: United Kingdom.
  • Device: Any device that can access the Service.
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service Provider: Any entity processing data on behalf of the Company.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data such as app usage metrics (note: MILOE does not collect this).
  • You: The individual using the Service.

2. What Data We Collect

MILOE does not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, or location data. The only data used by the app includes:

  • Journal entries (stored locally on your device).
  • App settings and preferences, including your opt-in status for AI processing.
  • Temporary transmission of journal content (anonymized) to our AI partner for affirmation generation.

3. How We Use Your Information

To generate personalized affirmations, MILOE uses an AI service ("Deep Seek API"). Your data is:

  • Sent securely and anonymously (no names, accounts, or device IDs).
  • Used once and not stored by MILOE or third parties.
  • Processed solely for generating affirmations.

4. AI Processing Agreement

You will be asked to accept an AI Processing Agreement on first use. This agreement explains:

  • How your journal data is used anonymously.
  • That you can opt out at any time via settings.
  • That opting out disables AI features, but journaling remains available.

5. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • MILOE does not upload or store your data on external servers.

6. Data Security

We prioritize data privacy and use best practices to protect your information:

  • Data sent to Deep Seek is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • No personal data is shared, stored, or sold.
  • Users remain in full control of their data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you are entitled to the following rights:

  • Access: You can view your journal entries and settings.
  • Deletion: You can delete all data directly from the app.
  • Opt-out: You can disable AI processing at any time.
  • No Discrimination: You will not be denied services for exercising your rights.

To exercise any rights or ask questions, contact us at info@sloffly.com

8. Do Not Sell My Personal Information (CCPA)

MILOE does not sell personal data. Since we do not collect identifiable information, no opt-out for "sale of data" is required under the CCPA.

9. No Tracking or Third-Party Analytics

We do not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Cookies
  • Advertising trackers

Your use of MILOE is private by design.

10. International Data Transfers

Although MILOE does not store personal data, anonymized journal text may be temporarily processed by third-party AI services (e.g., Deep Seek) hosted outside the UK or EU. These services follow appropriate data protection measures.

11. Children's Privacy

MILOE is not intended for use by children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has used MILOE, contact us and we will assist in deleting any related data.

12. Business Transfers

If MILOE is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, any locally stored user data remains under your control, as we do not retain copies.

13. Legal Compliance

We may disclose anonymized data if legally required to do so, e.g., to comply with a court order or legal process. However, since MILOE does not store personal data, the risk of such disclosure is minimal.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are significant, you will be notified in-app or via update notes. The "Last Updated" date will always reflect the latest version.

15. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns, contact us:
