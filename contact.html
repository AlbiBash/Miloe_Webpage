About MILOE

Our Mission

MILOE is a minimalist, elegant app designed to transform your thoughts into AI-personalized affirmations. Our mission is to help you cultivate confidence, strength, and positive energy through daily reflection and personalized encouragement.

How It Works

Using advanced AI technology, MILOE analyzes your journal entries to create meaningful, personalized affirmations. The app maintains your privacy by processing data locally and only sending anonymized content when generating affirmations.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All journal entries are stored locally on your device, and any data sent for affirmation generation is completely anonymized. We don't collect personal information, track usage, or share your data with third parties.

Our Values

MILOE is built on the principles of simplicity, privacy, and personal growth. We believe in creating tools that enhance well-being without compromising user privacy or adding unnecessary complexity to your life.
