About MILOE

Our Mission

MILOE is designed to help you transform your thoughts into positive affirmations through the power of AI. Our mission is to support your journey towards greater self-awareness and emotional well-being, one reflection at a time.

How It Works

Using advanced AI technology, MILOE analyzes your journal entries to create personalized affirmations that resonate with your experiences and emotions. The app maintains your privacy by processing data locally and only sending anonymized content when generating affirmations.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All journal entries are stored locally on your device, and any data sent for affirmation generation is completely anonymized. We don't collect personal information, track your usage, or share your data with third parties.

Our Values

MILOE is built on the principles of mindfulness, privacy, and personal growth. We believe in creating tools that respect your data while helping you cultivate a more positive and reflective mindset.
